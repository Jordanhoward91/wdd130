Trip Descriptions

Rapid Rush Adventure

Experience the thrill of Class IV rapids on our Rapid Rush Adventure. Perfect for thrill-seekers, this trip will get your adrenaline pumping as you navigate through intense rapids and breathtaking scenery.

Scenic Float

For those looking for a more relaxed adventure, our Scenic Float offers a calm and picturesque journey down the river. Ideal for families and first-time rafters, this trip provides stunning views and opportunities for wildlife spotting.

Family Fun Rafting

Our Family Fun Rafting trip is designed for all ages and skill levels. Enjoy a mix of gentle rapids and peaceful stretches, perfect for creating lasting memories with your loved ones.
